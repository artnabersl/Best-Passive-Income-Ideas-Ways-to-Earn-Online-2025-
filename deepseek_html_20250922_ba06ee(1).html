What Is Passive Income?

Passive income refers to earnings derived from a rental property, limited partnership, or other enterprise in which a person is not actively involved. Unlike active income, passive income continues to generate revenue with minimal ongoing effort.

Common types of passive income include:

  • Dividend stocks and investments
  • Royalties from intellectual property
  • Rental income from real estate
  • Affiliate marketing websites
  • Digital product sales
  • Online courses and membership sites
