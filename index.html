What Is Passive Income?

Passive income refers to earnings derived from a rental property, limited partnership, or other enterprise in which a person is not actively involved. Unlike active income where you trade time for money, passive income streams continue to generate revenue with minimal ongoing effort after the initial setup.

There are several types of passive income:

  • Investment Income: Dividends, interest, capital gains
  • Digital Products: Ebooks, online courses, software
  • Royalties: From books, music, patents, or licensing
  • Affiliate Marketing: Commission from promoting products
  • Rental Income: Physical or digital property rentals
